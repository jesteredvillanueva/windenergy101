Step 1 of 7 · Sun ☀️

It All Starts with the Sun!

The Sun heats the Earth — but land warms up faster than the ocean. That difference makes the air start to move.

🌬️ Swipe the cool ocean air across to the warm land!
Step 2 of 7 · Air 💨

Wind is Born!

Hot air over the land rises up high. Cool ocean air rushes in to fill the gap — and that moving air is wind!

🌀 Drag on the blades to spin them!
Step 3 of 7 · Wind Turbine Generator 🌪️

The Blades Spin!

The wind pushes the giant blades, and they spin ’round and ’round. The spinning blades harness wind energy.

⚡ Tap the generator to turn spinning into electricity!
Step 4 of 7 · Nacelle ⚙️

Inside the Nacelle!

Inside the turbine's head, the spinning blades turn a generator. The generator changes movement into electricity!

🔌 Trace the line from the turbine all the way to the house!
Step 5 of 7 · Transmission Lines 🗼

Electricity Travels Far!

Wires carry the electricity from the turbine, through the substation and transmission towers, all the way to town.

⚡ Tap the transformer 3 times to send power to the house!
Step 6 of 7 · Distribution ⚡

Clean Power Delivered Safely!

Before reaching homes, electricity passes through distribution utilities like Meralco. Think of them as sorting centers for electricity!

🏠 Tap the houses to light up the whole town!
Step 7 of 7 · Home 🏡

Your Home is Powered!

The electricity reaches homes and streetlights and everything lights up. You powered the town with wind energy!
